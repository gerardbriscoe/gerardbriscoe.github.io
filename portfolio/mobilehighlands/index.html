Mobile Highlands

2016

LTE, 3G,
I miss thee, GPRS,
Edge, so despise thee.

Critical Reflection

My first Haiku modified from the original longer version. I felt the format would be best supported by an explanatory image, which I subsequently also applied to the original poem.

While 5G was not available anywhere in the UK when I wrote this, and remains unavailable in the highlands. In time when it becomes more widely used, I would likely feel the need to update the Haiku.
